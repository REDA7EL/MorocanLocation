Hilton Garden Inn Casablanca Sud

Hilton Garden Inn Casablanca Sud sits in Sidi Maarouf, 22 km from the airport and near major landmarks. Opened in 2022, it has 155 rooms, an outdoor pool, gym, and meeting spaces. Guests enjoy Moroccan and international dining, free Wi-Fi, secure parking, and a digital key. El Patio Restaurant

El Patio Restaurant is a stylish dining spot inside Hilton Garden Inn Casablanca Sud, offering a mix of Moroccan and international dishes. Guests enjoy fresh, local flavors in a modern, welcoming space. The restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner with an à la carte menu and buffet options. Prince Moulay Abdellah Stadium - Rabat

Prince Moulay Abdellah Stadium in Rabat is a major sports venue with a capacity of about 53,000 seats. Opened in 1983, it hosts football matches, athletics, and big events like the Moroccan Throne Cup. The stadium is home to AS FAR and has modern facilities and a running track. It’s a landmark for sports fans and large gatherings in Morocco’s capital.View more samples click here

Smart Commute

Smart Commute is an eco-friendly service using electric tramways, busways, and buses to make commuting easier and greener.

BUS

Moroccan city buses offer cheap, easy city travel. They link neighborhoods to key areas.

Indriver

inDrive in Morocco offers flexible, affordable ride-hailing. Passengers negotiate fares directly with drivers.

TramWay

Moroccan tramways are clean, modern city transport. They connect key areas and cut traffic.

Al Boraq

The "Al Boraq" high-speed train in Morocco is Africa's first TGV, connecting Tangier and Casablanca at speeds up to 320 km/h.

CasaBusWay

Busway in Morocco provides fast, electric bus transport. It links busy areas with dedicated lanes.

Grand Taxi

The “grand taxi” in Morocco is a traditional shared transport that operates between cities or distant neighborhoods, usually carrying 6 passengers in large cars like Mercedes or Dacia.
